About Cell

Government Polytechnic College, Kaduthuruthy always had stood in the forefront in serving the societ as a technical institution providing quality skill education for the last 5 decades. The institution had contributed very much to the development of the locality as most of the alumni are employed with key role in technical departments of central, state and public sector establishments. The institute had contributed very much to the establishment of Kaduthuruthy. Established in the year 1999 as Govt. Polytechnic College under Department of Technical Education, Government of Kerala and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. nestled among lush greenery, of Appanchira Panchayath, in Border of Kottayam District. Situated at the middle of both industrial and tourist areas, the college provides a pleasant atmosphere for the students and is well connected. The infrastructure facilities are consistently being upgraded.
